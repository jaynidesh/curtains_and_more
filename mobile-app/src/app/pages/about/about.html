About

Le Club has been set up in 2021 by Fonciera Lifestyle Ltd, a subsidiary company of Fonciera Group. The Group focuses on real estate investment, promotion, development and hospitality.

With a growing property development portfolio and long established business contacts and partners, Fonciera created Le Club to emphasise on the Group's core spirit; “Live & Belong.”

Initially founded for the Group's business network contacts, Le Club is now open to all Mauritians and expats holding a residence permit in Mauritius.

Le Club members belong to a community inspiring to live life to the fullest whilst enjoying preferential rates, access to world class privileges and leisure activities, invitations to private events and networking events.
